Account and Data Deletion - MyApp

You can request deletion of your account directly from within the app.

Steps for Consumers

  1. Open the app.
  2. Go to the 4th tab (Consumer tab).
  3. Select Settings.
  4. Tap Remove Account.
  5. Confirm in the dialog.

Steps for Providers

  1. Open the app.
  2. Go to the 4th tab (Provider tab).
  3. Select Settings.
  4. Tap Remove Account.
  5. Confirm in the dialog.

Once you confirm, your account will be deactivated immediately. All data will be securely stored for 1 year. During this period, you can restore your account by signing up again with the same phone number. After 1 year, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted.
